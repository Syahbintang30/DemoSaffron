Our Story

More than a restaurant,
it's a destination

Saffron Resto & Space is where the richness of Indonesian culinary tradition meets a refined, modern atmosphere. Nestled in Bojongsari, Depok, we've crafted a space where every detail — from the aroma of spice to the warmth of our fireplace — is designed to linger in memory.

Whether you're gathering for a family meal, a private event, or simply indulging in an evening alone, Saffron offers a sanctuary of flavor and serenity.
